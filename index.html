MONEY WATCH

Money Watch assists user doing market research by making programming more accessible. The individual will be able to create a process that are as simple as linear regress or as complicated as bleeding edge Artificial intelligence machine learning algorithm with similar effort. We will accomplish this by making modules that are repeatable with and shareable among colleagues as well as communities. These modules could receive some, do some operation, and finally output some new information. These would then be chained together to create a money making machine!

The individuals that are more business inclined and are not too comfortable with programming will feel at home at Money Watch. He will be able to complete his tasks by using the pre-built modules that can be purchased from the Money Watch marketplace and construct a process pipeline that fits his needs. Once he has built his desired process pipelines he can take his results and plum it through the graphics pipeline similar to that of the other modules to visualize the results.

If the user has some minor programming experience then the embedded integrated development environment, or IDE, will allow them to be able to write their own modules seamlessly. The power user, or developer will have detailed documentation available to them to help them extend and change the platform as they please.
